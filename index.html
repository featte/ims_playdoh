CERT RENEWAL ACTIVITY GUIDE

Pre-check
• Email ITSec with attached ZIP (leaf, CA-ROOT, key).
• Upload cert & key to relevant directory before activity starts.

30 mins before
• Communicate in WA group & send email 15–30 min before start.
• Inform ITSec to upload cert on their site (F5).
• IMS can do cert renewal in DR first.
• Once ITSec finishes upload, proceed with PROD cert renewal.

Activity end
• Check new cert once ITSec uploads.
• After PROD renewal, monitor transactions.
• Inform WA group & send email with cert expiry + CMS trx screenshot.

Post-check
• Ask NOC to check relevant cert on Nagios monitoring.
• Upload cert screenshot & directory path in CMB ticket including:
  – ACS access log link
  – Cert in directory screenshot
  – Latest transaction screenshot
  – Complete email PDF
  – ITSec F5 screenshot
  – New cert viewer screenshot
